Visit New Orleans!

New Orleans is filled with Creole and Cajun food, live jazz, street performers, history, beautiful architecture, and a famous appreciation for all the temptations of life. Life in the Big Easy is lived well.

Food

The New Orleans food scene is dominated by Cajun or creole cuisines. Creole cuisine—a blend of French, West African, Spanish, Haitian, American, German and Italian influences—is a style of cooking that originated in Louisiana.

Music

The city’s music is its beating heart—especially true during Mardi Gras, Jazz Fest and other celebrations. The musical notes of jazz, brass, R&B, soul and many mixes fill the air along with night–blooming jasmine and other fabulous tropical fragrances.

Art

Local artists, many of whom are nationally and internationally known, abound in the French Quarter. You'll discover incomparable original paintings, photography, sculpture and other artwork while browsing distinctive shops on Royal Street and throughout the historic Vieux Carré.
